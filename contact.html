FAQ

Common Questions

How can I confirm my donation was received? ＋
After donating, you will receive a confirmation email within a few minutes. For EVC Plus / Sahal donations, please send your transaction receipt to donate@masjidsunnah.so or WhatsApp us.
Is my donation 100% used for construction? ＋
Yes. 100% of all donations go directly to construction costs. Administrative expenses are covered separately by the organizing committee. You can request a Transparency Report at any time.
Can I donate on behalf of a deceased loved one? ＋
Absolutely. This is a blessed act of Sadaqah Jariyah. In the message field when donating, simply write the name of the person you are donating on behalf of, e.g. "On behalf of the soul of [Name]." We will include them in our du'a.
How do I volunteer for the project? ＋
We welcome volunteers for fundraising, social media, logistics, and on-site support. Use the Contact Form above and select "Volunteering" as your subject, or WhatsApp us directly.
