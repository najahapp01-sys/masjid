Our Project

Building Masjid Sunnah
in Mogadishu

Masjid Sunnah is a community-driven mosque construction project in the heart of Mogadishu, Somalia. Our goal is to provide a spiritual sanctuary, an educational center, and a gathering point for the Muslim community — for generations to come.

Every brick laid, every pillar raised, is an act of Sadaqah Jariyah — a continuous charity whose reward flows even after we depart from this world. Join us in this noble cause.

🕌
Capacity for 500+ Worshippers Spacious prayer hall designed to serve the growing community of Mogadishu.
📚
Islamic Education Center Dedicated rooms for Quran classes, lectures, and youth programs.
🌊
Full Wudu Facilities Modern ablution area to accommodate worshippers at all prayer times.
